## 3D Printable Parts (STLs)

This directory contains all 3D-printable components required to assemble the thrust-vectoring rocket **test rig**.

All parts were designed for bench testing, prototyping, and system validation.

---

### Gimbal Components (`/gimbal`)
These parts form the 2-axis thrust-vectoring gimbal assembly:
- Yaw and pitch plates
- Servo arms and horn interface
- Bearing-side arm
- Motor mount

---

### Rocket Body Components (`/rocket_body`)
These parts form the structural body and mounting system:
- Rocket body tube and base
- Internal support rings
- Hatch and wire blocker
- Nose cone

---
